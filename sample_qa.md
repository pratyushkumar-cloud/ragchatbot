# Sample Q&A for SBI Mutual Fund FAQ Chatbot

**Product Context:** Designed for Groww platform users comparing SBI Mutual Fund schemes.

## Query 1
**Question:** What is the expense ratio of SBI Small Cap Fund?

**Answer:** The expense ratio of SBI Small Cap Fund is approximately 2.25% for direct plans and 2.50% for regular plans, as per the latest factsheet.

**Source:** https://www.sbimf.com/docs/default-source/scheme-factsheets/sbi-small-cap-fund-factsheet.pdf

---

## Query 2
**Question:** What is the exit load for SBI Blue Chip Fund?

**Answer:** SBI Blue Chip Fund has an exit load of 1% if redeemed within 1 year from the date of allotment. No exit load is applicable after 1 year.

**Source:** https://www.sbimf.com/docs/default-source/kim-sid/sbi-blue-chip-fund-kim.pdf

---

## Query 3
**Question:** What is the minimum SIP amount for SBI Equity Hybrid Fund?

**Answer:** The minimum SIP amount for SBI Equity Hybrid Fund is ₹500 per month, with no maximum limit.

**Source:** https://www.sbimf.com/docs/default-source/scheme-factsheets/sbi-equity-hybrid-fund-factsheet.pdf

---

## Query 4
**Question:** What is the lock-in period for SBI ELSS Fund?

**Answer:** SBI ELSS Fund has a mandatory lock-in period of 3 years from the date of investment, as per ELSS (Equity Linked Savings Scheme) regulations.

**Source:** https://www.sbimf.com/en-us/investor-education/sip

---

## Query 5
**Question:** How can I download my capital gains statement from SBI Mutual Fund?

**Answer:** You can download your capital gains statement by logging into your SBI Mutual Fund account, navigating to the 'Statements' section, and selecting 'Capital Gains Statement'. You can also request it through the customer service helpline or by visiting the nearest SBI Mutual Fund branch.

**Source:** https://www.sbimf.com/en-us/investor-education/how-to-download-statements

---

## Query 6
**Question:** What is the riskometer rating for SBI Small Cap Fund?

**Answer:** SBI Small Cap Fund has a riskometer rating of "Very High Risk" as it primarily invests in small-cap companies which are subject to higher volatility.

**Source:** https://www.sbimf.com/docs/default-source/scheme-factsheets/sbi-small-cap-fund-factsheet.pdf

## Query 8
**Question:** What is the benchmark for SBI Equity Hybrid Fund?

**Answer:** The benchmark for SBI Equity Hybrid Fund is CRISIL Hybrid 50+50 - Moderate Index, which is used to measure the fund's performance.

**Source:** https://www.sbimf.com/docs/default-source/scheme-factsheets/sbi-equity-hybrid-fund-factsheet.pdf
